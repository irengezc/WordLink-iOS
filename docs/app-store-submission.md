## Description Draft

WordLink is a word puzzle game about finding the hidden connections between
English phrases.

Each puzzle is a 9-word chain. You see the first word, then guess the next word
that completes a familiar compound phrase or expression. Every correct answer
reveals another link in the chain.

Why play WordLink:

- Solve quick 9-word chains.
- Choose easy, medium, or hard difficulty.
- Use hints when you get stuck.
- Review explanations for each phrase after a puzzle.
- Build vocabulary and phrase fluency through play.

WordLink is designed for word-game fans and English learners who enjoy learning
through context instead of memorizing isolated words.
